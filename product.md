Product Concept Document: Unknot
1. Executive Summary
Unknot is a "zero-friction" capture and organization tool designed specifically for language learners. Unlike traditional note-taking apps that require manual organization, Unknot allows users to "dump" raw notes, fragments, and vocabulary in real-time. Using AI, the app later "unknots" this data—structuring, categorizing, and transforming it into active study materials like flashcards.


2. The Problem & Solution
The Problem: During a fast-paced language class, students often scribble notes in a "data graveyard" (like a messy Google Doc). These notes are rarely reviewed because they lack structure and require too much effort to turn into study tools.
The Solution: A "stream-of-consciousness" interface that prioritizes speed of capture, followed by an automated AI processing layer that handles the heavy lifting of organization and lesson creation.


3. Core Features
3.1. The "Live Buffer" (Capture Phase)
Minimalist Stream: A clean, chronological text area. No folders, tags, or formatting required during the input phase.
High-Speed Entry: Optimized for desktop "side-car" use (next to a Zoom/Meet window) and mobile "quick-tap" entry.
Session Tagging: Optional ability to group a series of notes under a session name (e.g., "German B2 - Unit 4").
3.2. The "Unknotting" Engine (AI Processing)
Automated Parsing: Detection of language, parts of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives), and grammar patterns from raw text.
Data Enrichment: AI automatically adds:
Genders (der/die/das) and Plural forms.
Verb conjugations and prepositional requirements.
Phonetic IPA transcriptions.
Smart Categorization: Grouping notes into logical buckets (e.g., "Business Vocabulary," "Dative Case," "Idioms").
3.3. The "Retention" Suite (Study Phase)
Instant Flashcards: One-click generation of Spaced Repetition (SRS) cards based on the "Unknotted" notes.
Contextual Examples: AI generates natural sentences using the user's specific notes to show how words are used in real life.
Audio Generation: High-quality text-to-speech for all captured vocabulary to aid pronunciation.


4. User Workflows
Workflow A: The Live Student
Capture: During a 60-minute Italian lesson, the user types fragments: "andante - walking", "ho fame - I am hungry".
Process: After class, the user clicks "Unknot."
Review: The app presents a structured summary: 1 new verb, 1 idiomatic expression.
Study: The app immediately adds these to the user's daily flashcard deck.
Workflow B: The Casual Reader
Capture: While reading a German news article, the user copies/pastes 5 confusing sentences into the Unknot buffer.
Process: The AI breaks down the grammar in those sentences.
Refine: The user keeps the words they want to learn and discards the rest.


5. Value Proposition (Monetization)
Free Tier: Unlimited raw note-taking (The Buffer) and basic history.
Pro Tier:
Full AI "Unknotting" (Categorization & Enrichment).
Automated Flashcard generation and SRS tracking.
Native-speaker audio clips.
Multi-device sync.


6. Future Roadmap
Image-to-Note: Take a photo of a whiteboard/textbook and have the AI extract and "unknot" the text.
Browser Extension: Highlight text on any website to send it directly to your Unknot buffer.
Export Integration: One-click export to Anki, Quizlet, or Google Sheets for power users.
